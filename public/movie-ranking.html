The Best Movies According to Johann

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Founder

This is the story of how the Mc Donald's was founded and how Ray Croc influenced the whole fast food industry. Similar to the pursuit of happiness, Ray Croc displayed persistence inorder to achieve great heights.

The Social Network

Since I started my programming journey, this has been a gem of a movie every developer or programmer should watch. This is the story of Mark Zuckerberg when he created the very famous app called Facebook which has millions if not billions of users.

The Pursuit of Happiness

If your stuck in life, and your back against the wall, and everything just doesn't seem to work, this movie is for you. Will Smith displayed the power of persistence and perseverance in order for you to achieve your goals. Nothing good in life comes easy and this is by far the best representation of what achieving goal is all about.
